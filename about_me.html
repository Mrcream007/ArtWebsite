Get to know
me!

Welcome to my world of hyper-realistic portraiture, where every detail tells a story.
I’m Chukwuka Okocha, a dedicated portrait artist based in Nottingham, with a passion for
capturing the essence of my subjects through the intricate art of realism.

My journey as an artist began in the heart of (West) Africa, a city rich in culture and creativity. From a young age, I was fascinated by the human form and the emotions it could convey. Over the years, I have honed my craft, developing a meticulous technique that allows me to breathe life into every stroke of the brush.

What sets my work apart is my commitment to detail. I strive to create portraits that are not just accurate representations, but deeply personal reflections of my subjects. Whether it’s the subtle play of light on skin, the depth of emotion in the eyes, or the intricate textures of hair and fabric, I aim to capture the unique beauty of every individual who graces my canvas

When I’m not at the easel, you’ll find me exploring the artistic communities of Nottingham, drawing inspiration from the city’s diverse culture and rich history. I’m also passionate about sharing my love for art, offering workshops and classes to budding artists eager to learn the intricacies of portraiture.

Thank you for visiting my site. I invite you to explore my portfolio and discover the stories behind each portrait. Whether you’re interested in commissioning a piece or simply want to chat about art, I’d love to hear from you.
